About Me

I’m a 9th grader at the Great Mills High School STEM academy. I have a passion for learning, innovating and push myself. No matter how hard the endeavor is, I’m ready to persevere and achieve them. After all lofty endeavors are proof of our ambitions. As a natural leader, I’m always ready to lead, help and support others. I love to design with CAD, code and create amazing inventions. I’m a firm believer in organization and using and effective GTD strategy.

I believe in a few basic principles. Firstly, failure is the first step to success. We all make mistakes and that's human. It's what we do with our failures. Whether we give up or use them to improve next time that determines us potential. Secondly, it’s never too late to learn. I believe that anyone can learn at any age, which is why I strive to always improve, whether that’s improving my coding skills or improving my writing skills. Lastly, I believe that an open mindset is key to making change in the world and improving. Only if we are open to accepting our mistakes and failures can we improve and get better.
